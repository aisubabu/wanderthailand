Travel Thailand


From the tropical islands & flavourful cuisine to the stunning temples & famed martial arts.
A traveler's favorite!

Bangkok

Bangkok, the charming, buzzing, huge, somewhat overwhelming capital of Thailand. Bangkok is the city where most travelers start their journey through Asia.

Koh Tao

Koh Tao has some spectacular beaches and bays. The waters around the island are crystal clear and ideal for swimming and snorkeling. It’s famous for its diving spots.

Krabi

Krabi is a postcard-worthy region in Thailand, with scenes of limestone karsts rising out of crystal clear oceans that border thick green forests.

Khao Sok

Khao Sok National Park is one of Thailand’s best-kept secrets. Wild animals like gibbons, elephants are just a few things that give you a unique experience of Thailand.

What to expect


Language: The official language in Thailand is Thai.
Currency: The currency of Thailand is the Thai Baht .
